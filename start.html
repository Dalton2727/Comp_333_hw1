The Point Budgeter

About

WesWings or Swings is one of the top locations for student dining on Wesleyan campus! Its easy to get lost in the delicious meals without paying attention to your declining points. We want students to be able to enjoy Swings meals without stressing about spending too many points.
The purpose of our site is to encourage and support students in budgeting their points. Just input your points through eAccounts and you can easily create a weekly budget and factor in which meals would allow you to stick to your predetermined budget all through the site!
Swings' operating times are between 11AM to 8PM. It is only open for the Fall and Spring semesters to follow Wesleyan's yearly schedule. Wesleyan University closing times can be found on the academic calendar.

Menu

Swings' menu can be found on their website. Here you can find their year-long static menu as well as keep up with their rotating specials!
