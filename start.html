The Point Budgeter

WesWings or Swings is one of the top locations for student dining on Wesleyan campus! Its easy to get lost in the delicious meals without paying attention to your declining points. We want students to be able to enjoy Swings meals without stressing about spending too many points.
This website will be about budgeting points. This website will allow you to input the amount of points that you have through eAccounts and show how many swings meals you will be able to purchase.
Swings' operating times are always 11AM to 8PM, but is only open when Wesleyan University is open, meaning Swings is closed for the summer. Wesleyan University closing times can be found on the academic schedule here
