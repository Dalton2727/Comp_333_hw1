About Us
We know its easy to get lost in the delicious meals around campus without paying attention to your declining points. We want students to be able to enjoy our plethora of on campus dining and grocery options without worrying about spending too many points.
The purpose of our site is to encourage and support students in budgeting their points. Just input your points through eAccounts and you can easily create a weekly budget and factor in which purchases would allow you to stick to your predetermined budget all through the site! Simply swipe and our app will update your profile with how many points you've spent and how much it deviates from your budget.
